Our technology

Cloud Foundry

Cloud Foundry is an enterprise development platform allowing the fastest route from code to feedback, while maximising portability across clouds. We work with customers who choose this technology — they understand the most important thing isn’t the platform, but the apps that run on it.

We help enterprises operate Cloud Foundry effectively, so they can forget about it and focus on their applications.

BOSH

BOSH can provision and deploy software over hundreds of VMs on any cloud infrastructure. It also performs monitoring, failure recovery, and software updates with zero-to-minimal downtime.

Today it is still the only tool on earth that can effectively manage the lifecycle of stateful, distributed systems at scale. It underpins everything we do for our platform customers.

Concourse

Concourse is a intuitive CI tool that makes ‘pipelines’ a first-class citizen. Pipelines are critical to our way of working, where we focus on creating feedback loops when developing software, to learn faster.

It’s completely stateless and deployable from code, so your pipeline can be stored in source control with everything else. We also use it to automate continuous deployment of platforms themselves, making operational changes safe, cheap and easy.
